Made my first makers mark!

Date: 0x34-0a-4

Yesterday I made my first maker's mark! :D My general concept of a maker's mark is a mark that I put on all my creations including UI software and 3D prints. My maker's mark design was inspired by the minimalist designs of medieval mason's marks which were used to signify which mason created which stonework in buildings to determine sallery and to self advertise.
